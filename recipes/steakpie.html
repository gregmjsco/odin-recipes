Steak Pie

Steak Pie served with peas and chips

Cook chunks of beef in oven and then serve under pastry with fresh peas and a portion of chips

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Pastry
  • Peas
  • Chips

Steps

  1. Cut beef into chunks and add to oven dish with gravy
  2. Place pastry on top of dish and place in oven for 1 hour
  3. Whilst dish in oven prepare peas and chips
  4. Once steak cooked, serve all together
