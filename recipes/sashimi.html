What is Sashimi?

Sashimi is a Japanese dish of thinly-sliced raw food, usually fish and seafood, but also sometimes other meats.

Popular Types of Sashimi

  • Katsuo (Bonito / Skipjack Tuna
  • Sake (Salmon)
  • Maguro (Bluefin Tuna)

Steps to prepare

  1. Get fish
  2. Wash wish
  3. Slice fish
  4. Serve
